Epic Description

As a freelancer,
I want to be clearly informed that uploaded certificates can be viewed by signed-in employers and that personal information should be concealed,

Reduces accidental sharing of personal data (addresses, DOB, NI numbers)

Lowers legal and compliance risk

Aligns with UK GDPR principles (data minimisation, informed consent)

Creates a clear audit trail of user acknowledgement
